Wedding Decorators in Jaipur

Indian weddings are famous around the world for their grandiosity, splendid wedding décor and celebratory ambience. Its grandeur is mesmerizing to look at. The most important part of this beautiful, picturesque wedding landscape is the wedding decorations. There are many wedding decorators in Jaipur that you can choose from. Since an Indian wedding involves a lot of ceremonies, you need proper and unique wedding decorations for your wedding, be it a sangeet décor, a reception décor or ring ceremony, it all needs to be beautiful and classy. Even if you want an intimate wedding with a small party, you do need the decorations done nicely. Some of the questions that might ease this process for you are listed down below.

How much wedding decorators typically charge?

Usually, wedding decorators charge anything in between Rs. 75,000 - Rs. 6+ lakhs with the amount varying on the style of decor you choose and the extent of decorations, ranging from tent decorations and the indoor décor to full outdoor décor. Jaipur is known for its grand royal weddings and hence the price range might go slightly up during peak wedding season. Most Jaipur wedding decorators offer overall decoration packages, but if you do search a bit, you can find wedding decorators in budget as well.

What are the latest wedding decoration trends?

This year has seen some of the best wedding trends and some of them are listed below:

  • A royal Rajasthani wedding – Book your favourite palace right now, because this trend is one of the favourites for Jaipur weddings. With a grand palace, lights galore and assorted flower decorations, this wedding decoration trend is perfect for a regal, royal wedding. Fill it up with all the red and golden shades that you have and get your inner royalty out.
  • Bollywood themed wedding – Perfect for all the filmy fans and retro lovers. This wedding decoration trend starts with some of the best dialogues of Bollywood movies and an entire venue lit like a Bollywood party with quirky and stylish wedding décor.
  • Bohemian wedding decorations – Layering it up with all the bold colours that you have, this reception décor trend begins with colourful tent decorations, vibrant hangings on the wall with colourful fabric and vibrant flower decorations all around.

What are the questions to ask wedding decorators before booking them?

Since the outdoor décor is an absolutely irreplaceable part of your entire wedding ensemble, you cannot mess it up. It's better to know everything before making any decisions.

  • Work experience – How many years have you been into this business? This question is important because decorations are all about understanding and utilizing the space efficiently, which comes from experience.
  • Have you done my venue before? If yes, then there is nothing better than it. This way, the wedding decorators would know how to use the venue efficiently with the right decorations.
  • How many weddings do you do in a day? You want your marriage and sangeet décor to be perfect and unique. It's better to know if it clashes with any other wedding or not.

There are many wedding decorators in Jaipur that will most certainly match your budget and vision of the wedding. All you need to do is start selecting the theme you want and then discuss it with the right people, and you are good to go.
